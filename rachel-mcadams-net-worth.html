Rachel McAdams Net Worth: Lifestyle &amp; Career

Canadian actress and philanthropist Rachel Anne McAdams, known professionally as Rachel McAdams, has a net worth of $25 million.

She initially worked for Canadian films and TV productions. Similarly, she debuted in Hollywood with the 2002 film ‘Hot Chick.’

Rachel has appeared in more than 32 films to date, and her movies have grossed over $1.6 billion in the worldwide box office.

In 2015, she received the Academy Awards Best Actress nomination for her work in the drama/history film ‘Spotlight.’

Moreover, Glamour’s magazine dubbed Rachel the Sexiest Actress Alive in its November 2021 issue.

Rachel McAdams was born on 17th November 1978 in London, Ontario, Canada. Her father, Lance McAdams, was a truck driver and her mother, Sandra, was a nurse.

As a child, she began a passion for figure skating. Rachel competed in skating until she turned 18, winning regional awards.

Likewise, she would later embrace skating as a hobby. She has even stated that skating prepared her for physical acting.

Rachel McAdams | Lifestyle

Lifestyle

Even with such vast net worth, Rachel McAdams leads a pretty normal life. No celebrity extravaganza stuff!

She does, however, have a soft corner in her heart for finer things in life but does not flaunt it.

As for her daily lifestyle, she is an early bird. After waking up, she does exercise and, most importantly, yoga.

She is really passionate about biking and yoga. She practices Kundalini yoga nearly every morning.

Similarly, she once abandoned her non-vegetarian diet and instead began relying heavily on carbohydrates to get her energy.

However, animal-lover Rachel eventually gave up and returned to her carnivorous ways. She felt that her body started weakening without proper nutrition.

She had to banish her vegetarian diet after realizing her restricted eating habits left her too tired to work.

Her daily workout routine includes hip crossovers, scorpions, lateral lunges, sumo squat, forward lunges, and a 1.4-mile jog.

Following that, she does advance warmups that include squats, pushups, and jumping jacks.

And lastly, she does kettle ball swings, box jumps, and burpees.

Apart from that, Rachel has been spotted in designer clothes on several occasions.

For her clothing, her go-to brands are Miu Miu Matelassé Nappa Leather, Giuseppe Zanotti, Celine, and Louis Vuitton.

Rachel McAdams | Charity

Rachel McAdams is an environmentalist.

She is passionate about the causes related to the environment. In fact, she even launched the eco-friendly lifestyle website GreenSexy.org in 2007. She ran the website along with her two friends till 2011.

Even her house back in Toronto is powered by Bullfrog renewable energy.

Similarly, she visited and volunteered in Mississippi and Louisiana as part of the clean-up effort after Hurricane Katrina.

Likewise, McAdams appeared in promotional videos in 2013 for the Food and Water First Campaign.

Moreover, she worked with foundations like United Way of Canada, Sunshine Foundation of Canada, and Alzheimer’s Association.

She is also a member of Represent.Us, an anti-corruption organization, and also sits on their cultural council.

Furthermore, McAdams appeared in a video produced by Stand Earth in 2021. It called on the Canadian government to stop logging the rainforests across the British Colombia province.

Movies & TV Shows

Rachel made her television debut in 2001, appearing in an episode of the Disney Channel series ‘The Famous Jett Jackson’ and MTV pilot ‘Shotgun Love Dolls.’

With her debut, she booked roles in films My Name is Tanino, The Hot Chick co-starring Rob Schneider and Anna Faris, and Perfect Pie in 2002.

The same year she also guest-starred on the TV series Earth: Final Conflict and Guilt by Association.

In 2003 she appeared as Kate McNab on seven episodes of ‘Slings & Arrow.’

She got her major role in 2004 as Regina George in ‘Mean Girls,’ written by Tina Fey. The film grossed over $130 million at the box office and was also adapted into a Broadway musical.

The movie was a breakthrough not only for McAdams but also for other actresses, including Lindsay Lohan, Lacey Chabert, and Amanda Seyfried.

Moreover, Rachel’s 2004 film The Notebook was a massive hit that grossed over $116 million globally.

Following the success of The Notebook, she appeared in several other blockbuster hits, including ‘Wedding Crashers.’ Moreover, the movie grossed $288.5 million worldwide.

Similarly, she appeared in the 2005 film ‘Red Eye,’ which grossed $96.2 million. This was followed by comedy-drama ‘The Family Stone’ with a $92.9 million box office collection.

Apart from these, McAdams has several other movies in the pipeline, including ‘Doctor Strange in the Multiverse Madness.’ The movie is set to release in 2022.

Rachel McAdams | Career

Rachel was raised in a Protestant household (a form of Christianity) along with her siblings Kayleen and Daniel.

She learned to figure skate when she was four, participated in competitions, and also won regional awards.

McAdams went to Myrtle Street Public School and Central Elgin Collegiate Institute. She played soccer, volleyball, and badminton as a student.

Similarly, she was a member of the student council and Peer Helping Team.

The Canadian actress also attended several summer camps and started performing at the age of 12 with Original Kids Theater Company.

She not only appeared in the play but directed a few of them. Interestingly she received the acting award at the 1995 Sears Ontario Drama Festival.

After completing high school, McAdams attended York University in Toronto and obtained a Bachelors’s degree in Fine Arts in 2001.
